# Galactic Collective Intelligence

## Main Concept

The galaxy behaves as a dynamic geopolitical ecosystem.

The system should simulate:
- balance-of-power behavior
- collective reactions to hegemonies
- geopolitical paranoia
- reputation spread
- strategic opportunism
- alliance blocs

---

## Important Principle

AI empires are NOT isolated actors.

They react to:
- galactic trends
- dominant powers
- historical betrayals
- rising threats
- economic dominance
- military snowballing

---

## Multiplayer Illusion Goal

The galaxy should feel closer to:
- multiple human players

Rather than:
- isolated scripted bots

The project aims to create:
- social strategic pressure
- collective adaptation
- emergent diplomacy
- long-term geopolitical responses

---

## Anti-Hegemony Dynamics

If a civilization becomes dominant:
- fear spreads
- coalitions form
- rival powers react
- strategic balancing begins

This should emerge naturally.

NOT through hardcoded anti-player cheating.

---

## Galaxy Profiles

The galaxy itself may possess cultural tendencies.

Examples:
- Balanced Galaxy
- Dark Forest
- Cold War Galaxy
- Treacherous Galaxy
- Fanatic Galaxy

These influence:
- diplomacy
- aggression
- paranoia
- alliance stability
- betrayal frequency
